Eagle

The eagle is a bird of prey that is known for its keen eyesight and powerful flight. It is often considered a symbol of freedom and strength. Eagles are found in various parts of the world and are admired for their majestic appearance and hunting skills.

Cat

The cat is a small carnivorous mammal that is often kept as a pet. Cats are known for their agility, sharp claws, and independent nature. They are popular companions due to their playful behavior and affectionate demeanor.
